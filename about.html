From Seed to Sunflower

Learn how a seed becomes a beautiful flower!

Read: How a Seed Grows

A seed contains everything a plant needs to start growing. With water, soil, and sunlight the seed sprouts roots and a tiny shoot. Over time the plant grows leaves, gets taller, and finally opens a bright flower. Bees visit the flower, and new seeds can grow from it.
